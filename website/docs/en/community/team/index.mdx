Rsbuild follows the Semantic Versioning specification.

## Team

The development of Rsbuild is driven by ByteDance's Rspack team and community contributors.

For information about the Rspack team members, please refer to [Rspack - Core team](https://www.rspack.dev/misc/team/core-team).

For information about the Electron-Rspack team members, please refer to [Electron-Rspack - Core team](https://www.rspack.dev/misc/team/core-team).
